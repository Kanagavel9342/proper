Automatic Traffic Management System

Optimizing traffic flow, reducing congestion, and enhancing road safety with intelligent automation.

Traffic Management Challenges

  • Congestion: Wastes time, increases fuel consumption.
  • Safety Concerns: Inefficient flow increases accident risks.
  • Inefficient Resource Utilization: Static signals are ineffective.
  • Environmental Impact: Increased greenhouse gas emissions.

Proposed System

The system adjusts traffic signal timing dynamically based on real-time traffic density.

How IoT-Based ATC Works

  • Sensors and cameras collect real-time traffic data.
  • Algorithms analyze traffic patterns.
  • Signal timings are adjusted accordingly.
  • Authorities receive real-time alerts.
